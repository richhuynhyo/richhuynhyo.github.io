The MSI Creator 15 is a laptop for creators that sports an OLED (organic light-emitting diode) monitor display. I was tasked with creating video social posts to highlight the enhanced contrast and color fidelity of OLED.

With After Effects, I composited static product assets with stock footage to create 5 second looping videos for social media.
