Brighter Future For Gaming is an online giveaway campaign launched by MSI in cooperation with Intel, PC Master Race, and Blended Group. I was tasked with designing the key visual and promotion landing page.

For the key visual, I composited the various giveaway products on a futuristic skyline backdrop. I created faux reflections for product emphasis as well as to indicate cleanliness. After the key visual, I laid out the rest of the promotional page using soft light blues to carry a positive, utopian-future tonality.
