I was tasked with designing a webpage to market various MSI Creator products. The objective was described as 90% product marketing, 10% community building, and must include a catalogue of influencer/creator videos.

I coordinated with a product and channel marketers to identify the products the necessary products, features, and videos to showcase on the page. After requirements were established, I mocked up an interactive prototype with filler text in Adobe XD to demonstrate the planned content layout and overall visual design. After a few adjustments, the layout and visual design was approved. I then proceeded to provide copy direction to the marketers while simultaneously designing the web graphics and coding the page responsively.

The final design is minimalistic but uses eye-catching imagery in the product displays to express color and creativity. Consistent rounded rectangle buttons with hover animations denote interactive page elements. Videos and additional product/feature information are displayed with Javascript overlays, allowing key information to be seen initially with less clutter.
