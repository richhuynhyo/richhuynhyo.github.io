PROJECT

  • MSI Creator
    Dribble Animated Ad

ROLES

  • Art Direction
  • Video Composition
  • Motion Design

I was tasked with creating a looping/animated ad for MSI's Creator Series laptops to be launched on the creative platform, Dribble. As the sole designer on this project, I decided on a fun, energetic, colorful tone to inject a sense of "creative spirit" into the MSI Creator Series.

After having sourced and cut the funky hiphop background track, I utilized swift, poppy animations to give a dynamic feel. I used a bright color pallete with a mixed medium aesthetic to compliment the colorful energy of the music.
