PROJECT

  • MSI x Public Domain
    April Fool's Social Campaign

ROLES

  • Creative Direction
  • Graphic Design
  • Photo Manipulation

I collaborated with the MSI social media coordinator to create a humorous social post for April Fools 2022. Having observed a growth in popularity in brand crossovers, I came up the concept of a mock announcement post of a MSI crossover with the entirety of public domain works.

The social post consisted of an album that initially captures audience attention with legitimately attractive product-reskins. As the audience explores the album, they will be met with increasingly ridiculous applications of public domain art that develop into humorous absurdity.
