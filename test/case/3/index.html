Concept - VR User Interface

Applying Biomechanics to Virtual Reality Interfaces

UX Research, Prototyping

Objective:

Improve the vr user interface usability and comfort



Process:

  1. Observation
  2. Hypothesis
  3. User Personas
  4. Wireframe
  5. Prototype

Observation:

Shoulder to elbow pivot Eye tracking - veritcal vs horizontal saccades smooth pursuit

Hypothesis:

In an effort to improve customer experience on MSI online brand stores for various retail channels (Amazon, Newegg, etc), I initiated a redesign of the MSI brand store on Amazon, creating the slogan "Work, Play, Create, Build," to establish an encompassing theme for MSI's various product lines. In the redesign, I pushed this brand messaging through selected photography and typographic consistency.

Goal-directed Personas They want to adjust settings Control - Full mobility Limited mobility - Age, Injury Seated and standing
