PROJECT

  • Study & Play In Style

ROLES

  • Web Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Illustration

MSI marketing scheduled to run a web/social media sweepstakes to promote computer gaming peripherals for the back to school season. With the target audience being students and gamers, I was tasked with designing an energetic, youthful aesthetic for the campaign.

Key Visual

The campaign's key visual was primarily composed of geometric vectors to establish a clean and modern visual style. The bright, contrasting, yellow color scheme was chosen to establish youthful energy that is also reminiscent of school buses and composition books. The illustrated controller was used as a main graphic element due to its prominence as a gaming symbol.

Style

The style choices for the campaign's website were directly pulled from the key visual. Fonts, colors, and graphic elements were reused to maintain consistency in the website's design and the key visual.

Responsive Web Design

The campaign's website was responsively design to support mobile and desktop browsers. The content consists of the key visual, short intro, a list of prizes, instructions to enter, and an embedded sweepstakes form. Simple slide in animations were added to support the energetic, dynamic tone.

Social Media Banners

The key visual was reused to create banner ads and social graphics to promote the website and campaign.
