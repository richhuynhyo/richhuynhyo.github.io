PROJECT

  • Study & Play In Style

ROLES

  • Web Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Illustration

MSI marketing scheduled to run a web/social media sweepstakes to promote computer gaming peripherals for the back to school season. With the target audience being students and gamers, I was tasked with designing an energetic, youthful aesthetic for the campaign.

The campaign's website was responsively designed to support mobile and desktop browsers. The content consists of the key visual, short intro, a list of prizes, instructions to enter, and an embedded sweepstakes form. Content slide-in animation was added to support an energetic, dynamic tone.
