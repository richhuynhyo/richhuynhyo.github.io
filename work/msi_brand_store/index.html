In an effort to improve customer experience on MSI online brand stores for various retail channels (Amazon, Newegg, etc), I initiated a redesign of the MSI brand store on Amazon, creating the slogan "Work, Play, Create, Build," to establish an encompassing theme for MSI's various product lines. In the redesign, I pushed this brand messaging through selected photography and typographic consistency.

The brand store update was an ongoing process, with redesigns being rolled out to accommodate for new product releases. The home page was the first to be updated, and in the first month saw an +20% improvement in Orders/Views over the previous month. In 6 months, the page saw an average +60% improvement in Orders/Views per month YoY.
