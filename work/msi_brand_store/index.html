In an effort to improve customer experience on MSI online brand stores for various retail channels (Amazon, Newegg, etc), I redesigned the homepage of the MSI brand store on Amazon. The redesign would be used as a model for the MSI brand stores on other retailer websites.

Competitive Analysis

In the research phase of this project, I looked through competitor brand stores to identify effective aspects in their brand store strategy. The use of strong text elements with consistent tone allow the stores to deliver branded messaging while also serving as a product catalogue.

Defining Objectives

The original MSI brand store homepage (pictured below) was serving only as a product catalogue. Based on my competitive analysis, I decided that brand messaging would be important in the page refresh. The page refresh would also require the addition of new product lines and product categories. I would have to find a creative way to weave a brand message that connected the numerous products.

Slogan

In 2020 MSI introduced a new "Business and Productivity" product category for laptops, desktops, and monitors, creating a total 3 product categories: Gaming, Creator, Business and Productivity. In 2021, MSI launched a number of pc components: Liquid Coolers, Power Supplies, and Solid State Drives. To efficiently describe MSI's product offerings, I created a simple slogan "Work, Play, Create, Build" which would encompass the Business and Productivity, Gaming, Creator, and PC components respectively.

Style

I created a style guide to propagate the redesign throughout the MSI brand store's other pages. The font and colors are follow MSI's corporate brand style guide, but the tracking was tightened to energize the headline text. Strong and consistent text elements would be important to deliver the brand message.

Storytelling and Design

In order to effectively market the various products, I had to group product lines into categories that would appeal to certain audiences and then use photos and copy that would cater to that audience group. Strong headlines would establish and define the product groups when scrolling through the brand store.

Laptops are in its own group. I believed lifestyle-focused photography best highlighted the product's portability and design.

Desktops, monitors, and peripherals were grouped together as "Battlestation Ready Hardware," appealing to a group of computer enthusiasts that enjoy designing their computer desks setups.

PC cases and components were grouped together with photos showcasing their use in computer builds.
