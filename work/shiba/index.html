This was a creative experiment in utilizing a variety of creative techniques to design a seemingly illustrated, monster-movie poster.

The process begain with using various imported 3D models into Blender, and compositing them with added lights to create a scene. I rendered the models and layered them into Photoshop, where I used photobashing to fill in the missing details, overlayed additional light and particle effects, and added the title typography. I finally imported the working file into Topaz Studio, added an illustrative filter, and adjusted it into the final product.
