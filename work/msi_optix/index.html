The MSI Optix MPG321QRF-QD is a 32" Gaming Monitor that introduces improved color range with Quantum Dot technology. I was tasked with updating the product's key visual and etailer marketing content. The project called for greater focus on Quantum Dot technology while educating consumers on key gaming features such as Console Mode and Rapid IPS.

For the key visual, I went with rich colors and extruding effects to express dynamic immersion. Immediately under the key visual, I added a main feature summary panel to improve the consumer shopping experience. I followed with a series of comparison modules to demonstrate the improved visual experience of the Optix monitor compared to generic competitors.
