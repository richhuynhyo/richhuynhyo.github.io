PROJECT

  • REACH:
    Brand & Marketing

ROLES

  • Branding
  • Typography
  • Print Design

REACH is an educational program that assists current and former foster youth students of Mt. San Antonio College. They offer foster students a welcoming environment to get individual counseling, discover community resources, and participate in events and workshops.

I was tasked with creating brand and marketing materials to spread information and awareness of the program and their events.

Branding

Tasked with designing a marketing brochure, I created a moodboard to establish a creative direction. I drew inspiration from clouds, baseball, and slice-of-life school cartoons. The goal was to portray REACH as welcoming, hopeful, and youthful while maintaining its role as a university organization.

The warm blue color scheme is derived from a blue heart symbol, synonymous with foster care. Lastly with the REACH logo already being pre-established, I maintained its font for copy.

Typographic Slogan

To more efficiently spread awareness to the programs goals, I created and hand-drew a custom typographic slogan for use on branded materials. I decided to go with the direct message for the slogan text. The typography was inspired by baseball logos, intended to complement the visual identity established by the moodboard.

Branded Materials

Using my style guidelines and typographic slogan, I designed a number of branded materials to spread awareness of the REACH program and its goals.
