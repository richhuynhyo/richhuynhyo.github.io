PROJECT

  • REACH:
    Brand & Marketing

ROLES

  • Branding
  • Typography
  • Print Design

REACH is an educational program that assists current and former foster youth students of Mt. San Antonio College. They offer foster students a welcoming environment to get individual counseling, discover community resources, and participate in events and workshops.

I was tasked with creating brand and marketing materials to spread information and awareness of the program and their events. I created and hand-drew a custom typographic slogan for use on branded materials, utilizing a warm blue color scheme derived from the foster care, blue heart symbol.
