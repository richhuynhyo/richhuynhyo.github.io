Smash the Competition

Graphic Design, Digital Illustration - MSI

I was tasked with creating a comic-inspired poster highlighting unique features of MSI's new 9th Gen Intel® Series gaming laptops for the B&H Photo store. The features highlighted are the GE Raider's front cover RGB Lighting, GS65's cooling, and GT75's performance power.

The project intially required just the single laptop, but it eventually grew to require three. The color pallet also shifted from of an homage to retro comics to a brighter, more modern color pallet. Lastly, the "laser" effect on the GE Raider was changed to less obscure the actual RGB lighting on the product.

REACH : Brand Materials

Graphic Design, Digital Illustration - Mt.SAC REACH

I was tasked with developing a general marketing brochure for REACH Foster Youth program. The brochure was designed to be quickly describe the REACH program and provide contact details.

The light blue color pallet was chosen to correspond with the light blue ribbon used in foster care awareness. With a combination of REACH's photos and my own event photography, I used a photo collage to display REACH's fun, welcoming activities.
