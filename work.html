Study & Play In Style

Web Design, Graphic Design, Illustration

MSI marketing scheduled to run a web/social media sweepstakes to promote computer gaming peripherals for the back to school season. With the target audience being students and gamers, I was tasked with designing an energetic, youthful aesthetic for the campaign.

Key Visual

The campaign's key visual was primarily composed of geometric vectors to establish a clean and modern visual style. The bright, contrasting, yellow color scheme was chosen to establish youthful energy that is also reminiscent of school buses and composition books. The illustrated controller was used as a main graphic element due to its prominence as a gaming symbol.

Style

The style choices for the campaign's website were directly pulled from the key visual. Fonts, colors, and graphic elements were reused to maintain consistency in the website's design and the key visual.

Responsive Web Design

The campaign's website was responsively design to support mobile and desktop browsers. The content consists of the key visual, short intro, a list of prizes, instructions to enter, and an embedded sweepstakes form. Simple slide in animations were added to support the energetic, dynamic tone.

Social Media Banners

The key visual was reused to create banner ads and social graphics to promote the website and campaign.

MSI Brand Store

Storytelling, Copywriting, Web Design, Graphic Design

In an effort to improve customer experience on MSI online brand stores for various retail channels (Amazon, Newegg, etc), I redesigned the homepage of the MSI brand store on Amazon. The redesign would be used as a model for the MSI brand stores on other retailer websites.

Competitive Analysis

In the research phase of this project, I looked through competitor brand stores to identify effective aspects in their brand store strategy. The use of strong text elements with consistent tone allow the stores to deliver branded messaging while also serving as a product catalogue.

Defining Objectives

The original MSI brand store homepage (pictured below) was serving only as a product catalogue. Based on my competitive analysis, I decided that brand messaging would be important in the page refresh. The page refresh would also require the addition of new product lines and product categories. I would have to find a creative way to weave a brand message that connected the numerous products.

Slogan

In 2020 MSI introduced a new "Business and Productivity" product category for laptops, desktops, and monitors, creating a total 3 product categories: Gaming, Creator, Business and Productivity. In 2021, MSI launched a number of pc components: Liquid Coolers, Power Supplies, and Solid State Drives. To efficiently describe MSI's product offerings, I created a simple slogan "Work, Play, Create, Build" which would encompass the Business and Productivity, Gaming, Creator, and PC components respectively.

Style

I created a style guide to propagate the redesign throughout the MSI brand store's other pages. The font and colors are follow MSI's corporate brand style guide, but the tracking was tightened to energize the headline text. Strong and consistent text elements would be important to deliver the brand message.

Storytelling and Design

In order to effectively market the various products, I had to group product lines into categories that would appeal to certain audiences and then use photos and copy that would cater to that audience group. The products groups are then ordered by audience size and technical knowledge (mass market to enthusiasts). Strong headlines would establish and define the product groups when scrolling through the brand store.

Laptops

Laptops are in its own group. I created the subehader "portable performance" and utilized lifestyle-focused photography to highlight the product's portability, design, and processing power. The laptops also present the various product categories: Gaming, Business & Productivity, and Content Creation, which correlates to the slogan.

Desktops, Monitors, and Peripherals

Desktops, monitors, and peripherals were grouped together as "Battlestation Ready Hardware," appealing to a group of computer enthusiasts that enjoy designing their computer desks setups. This product group would be an opportunity to display different types of "battlestations" to draw up inspiration for these enthusiasts.

Cases and Components

PC cases and components were grouped together with photos showcasing their use in computer builds. This product group has the simplest design, as the main focus is to display the various PC component in an orderly faster. This is essentially a product catalogue for technical users.
