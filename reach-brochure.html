REACH Brochure Revision


Requirements and Goals

  • Tri-Fold Brochure 8.5in x 11in
  • Primary color must be blue.
  • Appeal to college students, but still appear professional.
  • Establish positive, hopeful, welcoming feel.
  • Include lots of photos.

Concept Development

1
Inspiration

I sought inspiration from baseball and positive portrayals of schools in cartoons. Clouds were a inspirational factor for their hopeful symbolism.

2
Compositional Sketch

Influenced into establishing a mood of hopeful comfort, I started sketching a general content layout and overarching movement.

Asset Production

3
Custom Typography

I designed a custom typographic asset for the brochure and future use. The typography was inspired by the optimistic, youthful, established connotation of baseball team fonts.

4
Photography

I took photos at various REACH events. The focus was to capture genuine, positive human interaction.

Design Implementation

5
Prototype

I created a rough draft for initial approval.

6
Refinement

After the roughs were approved, I moved began working and refining the final version. The original inside-design was adapted into a collage format when many of the provided photos were of low resolution.

Final Product
